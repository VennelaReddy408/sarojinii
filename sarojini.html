Biography

Sarojini Naidu (1879-1949) was an Indian poet and political activist who played an important role in the Indian independence movement against the British Raj12345. She was the first woman to become the President of the Indian National Congress and the first woman Governor of Uttar Pradesh35. She was a proponent of civil rights, women's emancipation, and anti-imperialism14.

Achievements

First woman President of the Indian National Congress.
First female Governor of an Indian state.
Recipient of the prestigious Kaiser-i-Hind Medal.
Dedicated her life to the Indian independence movement.
Played a key role in the Civil Disobedience Movement and the Quit India Movement.
